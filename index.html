💾 Backup

Download a snapshot of all your inventory data to your computer. The backup is a ZIP file containing three files:

  • inventory_backup_FULL_*.json — complete data including products catalog and all events. Suitable for full disaster recovery.
  • sales_*.csv — all sales as a flat spreadsheet (opens in Excel, LibreOffice, Google Sheets). Suitable for accounting and analysis.
  • stock_in_*.csv — all stock receipts as a flat spreadsheet.

Backups are read-only — they don't change anything on the server. Recommended: store the ZIP somewhere safe periodically (your computer, cloud drive, USB stick).
